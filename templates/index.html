What is diabetes?

A chronic condition affecting how your body turns food into energy. Early lifestyle changes can delay or prevent onset.

Major Risk Factors

Factors that increase your diabetes risk

Poor Sleep

Less than 6 hours of sleep disrupts glucose metabolism and increases insulin resistance.

High Stress

Chronic stress elevates cortisol levels, leading to increased blood sugar and weight gain.

Unhealthy Diet

High intake of processed foods, sugary drinks, and refined carbs accelerates diabetes risk.

Sedentary Life

Lack of physical activity reduces insulin sensitivity and promotes weight gain.

Prevention Starts Today

Simple lifestyle changes can make a significant difference

Stay Active
  • Aim for 30 minutes of moderate activity daily
  • Take stairs instead of elevators
  • Include strength training twice a week
  • Break up sitting time every hour
Eat Smart
  • Choose whole grains over refined carbs
  • Include plenty of vegetables and fruits
  • Limit sugary drinks and processed foods
  • Practice portion control
Prioritize Sleep
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Avoid screens before bed
Manage Stress
  • Practice mindfulness or meditation
  • Engage in hobbies and relaxation
  • Maintain social connections
  • Seek support when needed
